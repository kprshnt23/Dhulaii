Our Services

“We are the perfect Valet to keep your car clean”

DHULAII...

DHULAII is a prominent hand car wash company which has started its services from Durg, Chattisgarh under the Umbrella of RLV TECH AND SERVICES LLP and, soon to launch its services in cities like Bangalore, Mumbai, Ahmedabad and Delhi NCR..
Our concept is Old School but the approach is New, as we provide the best car wash experience at your door step any one can have. We know you want your car to shine before you drive it, but with your tight schedule. That’s why we provide you round the clock service at your door step.
